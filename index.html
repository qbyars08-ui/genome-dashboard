⚠️ Clinical Significance

APOE ε3/ε4 carriers have approximately 3x the population average risk of developing late-onset Alzheimer disease. Lifetime risk increases from ~11% (general population) to ~25-30%. This is the single strongest common genetic risk factor for AD. However, many ε4 carriers never develop the disease — lifestyle factors (exercise, cognitive engagement, cardiovascular health, sleep quality) significantly modulate expression. As an ER physician, you are likely aware that APOE4 also affects lipid metabolism, with carriers showing higher LDL cholesterol on average.
